Scrum - Agile Development

Written by Pepper Targaryen with ♥ on March 2, 2020 in 管理

Scrum is one of the most popular ways to implement Agile. In this iterative approach, there are Sprints that last one to two weeks and allow the team to deliver software on regular basis. Scrum uses a software model that follows a set of roles, responsibilities, and meetings.

What is Agile?

To respond to this question, we need to learn first what is a traditional software development cycle, Waterfall. Waterfall is a sequential development cycle including following phases: Plan, Build, Test, Review and Deploy. The biggest problem of this development cycle is that it takes too much time. After going through these five phases, the product is probably out of date, which will a great loss to the company.

要回答这个问题，我们需要先了解一下传统的软件开发流程——瀑布式开发流程。瀑布式开发流程包含Plan、Build、Test、Review和Deploy这5个阶段。这种开发流程的最大问题就是时间开销太大，当完成5个阶段之后，所开发的产品非常有可能已经过时了。

Agile software development methodology is more adaptable to changes. A constant feedback from the end users is encouraged. In Agile, there is an incremental and iterative development approach. The work is prioritized on the basis of business or customer value. There are cross-functional teams that work on the iterations of the product over a period of time. Each iteration is focused on producing a working product.

敏捷开发对于变化更加适应，客户的需求反馈会被开发团队纳入计划中，产品会被定义为多个版本，每个版本都一个可以交付的产品，每次产品的迭代将会带来增量更新。

What is Scrum

Scrum is the most popular approach to implement Agile. It helps to manage software development with an iterative approach. There are fixed-length iterations known as a Sprint that allows shipping software frequently. A Sprint lasts one to two weeks and at the end of each Sprint, the stakeholders and team members conduct a meeting to plan the next steps.

Scrum是最普遍的敏捷开发框架，软件开发使用迭代更新，可以快速交付产品，每个迭代称之为一个Sprint。每个Sprint花费1至2周，每个阶段结束的时候董事局和开发团队召开一个会议来计划下一步。

The roles, responsibilities, and meetings are fixed in a Scrum. In each Sprint, there is Sprint planning, daily stand-up, Sprint demo and Sprint retrospective. There are task boards and burndown charts to follow up on the progress of the Sprint as well as to receive incremental feedback.

Roles in a Scrum

Product Owner

Product Owner (PO) not only focuses on the work to be completed but also focuses on business and market requirements. The PO interacts with the team as well as other stakeholders to build and manage the Product Backlog. The role of a PO is to motivate the team to align them with the goal and vision of the project.

产品责任人根据商业和市场需要，编写Product Backlog，定义需要完成的任务，他要激励开发团队完成工程目标。

Scrum Master

Scrum Master is responsible for organizing meetings, dealing with challenges and bottlenecks. The Scrum Master interacts with Product Owner to ensure that the Product Backlog is ready for the next Sprint. He or she is also responsible to ensure that the team follows the Scrum process.

敏捷专家负责组织会议、解决难题，他会和产品责任人交流，保证产品任务手册的制定，并负责保证团队的开发进度。

Scrum Team

The Scrum Team can be comprised of 5 to 7 members. In a Scrum team, there are no distinct roles as a programmer, designer or tester rather everyone has a set of tasks that they complete together. The Scrum Team plans the amount of work they can complete in each iteration.

敏捷团队大概5到7人，团队成员并非有一个具体的角色，每个人都可以是程序员、设计者或测试员，团队一起完成一项工作。

Workflow

Product Backlog

The Product Backlog comprises a list of all the desired features of the product. The Product Owner and Scrum Master prioritize the items on the basis of user stories and requirements. The development team refers to the Product Backlog to complete the task during each Sprint.

产品任务手册定义了产品的特点和开发任务，产品责任人和敏捷专家需要将这些任务按重要性排序，团队根据该文件开发。

Sprint Planning

In the Sprint planning meeting, the Product Owner provides a list of high priority items on the backlog. The team chooses the task they can complete during the Sprint and transfer the tasks from Product Backlog to the Sprint Backlog.

Sprint Backlog

Sprint Backlog defines features and tasks for a Sprint. After the Sprint planning meeting, Sprint Backlog should be confirmed.

Sprint

This lasts 1 to 2 weeks, where Scrum team develops the product. Every day holds a Daily Scrum meeting to discuss what’s done yesterday and today’s tasks.

Potentially Shippable Product

When a Sprint terminates. a potentially shippable product will be produced. Two meetings should be held after a Sprint.

Spring Review

A live demonstration is given at the end of each Sprint to showcase the work the team has completed during the Sprint in the Sprint review meeting.

Retrospective

This meeting is held to reflect on the success of the Scrum process and is there any changes required to be made in the next Sprint. The team discusses the highs and lows of the earlier Sprint and all the improvements for the next Sprint.

Summary

Agile is the software development methodology that focuses on customer satisfaction by delivery shippable software frequently. Scrum is one of the many approaches to implement Agile. Scrum is suitable for certain type of projects where there are rapidly changing requirements.

Reflection

Scrum still could suffer from various problems in real projects. Inconfidence, lack of passion…

敏捷开发在实际过程中也许会遭遇各种各样的问题，产品责任人和开发团队之间的不信任，开发团队缺乏工作热情，产品责任人没有好好调研市场需求，天马行空的任务，等等……
